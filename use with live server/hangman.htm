Hangman is a game where players have to guess a word based off of its length and a hint (optional). You have a limited number of tries, depending on the difficulty of the game. The game ends once the player(s) guess the word or they run out of guesses.

There are lots of customizable settings for Hangman on FunkyArithmeticGames, such as choosing a word or getting a randomly generated one, controlling the number of lives, and whether a hint should be given. Have fun by yourself or with your friends!

Note: Guessing an incorrect word will result in 2 lives being taken.

Note: Entering custom lives does not allow you to witness my beautiful artwork. Choose wisely.
